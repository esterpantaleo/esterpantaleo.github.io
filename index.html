Ester Pantaleo

github.com/esterpantaleo

I'm a data scientist with a PhD in Physics from the University of Bari, Italy, interested in data visualization. I have done research in different fields, from Physics to Genomics and Econometrics, trying to model and simulate different systems using Bayesian Statistics and Monte Carlo methods. Recently I started developing data visualizations.


In this website I'm collecting some projects I have developed as a researcher or as a freelancer. Currently I'm developing etytree an interactive tool for the visualization of the etymology of words funded by an IEG grant of the Wikimedia Foundation. If you have comments please do not hesitate to contact me at esterpantaleo at gmail dot com.


I am also an aspiring fine artist, painter, sculptor. If you are curious you can check my personal website here. I'm on the job market right now and interested in full time or part time remote work.

  1. etytree

    Website · Github · Bitbucket · grant · demo

    An interactive tool to visualize the etymology of words and to find etymologically connected words. Developed with an individual engagement grant from the Wikimedia Foundation.

  2. ANPR dashboard

    Website

    A real time interactive dashboard showing progress in the construction of a National Resident Population Register. Developed for the Digital Transformation Team of the Italian Government.

  3. Star cluster evolution

    Website · Github

    An animated and interactive version of the HR diagram that evolves in time from birth to an age of 5 billion years made for the Adler Planetarium, Chicago, using simulated data of a cluster of 100-star similar to our Sun's birthplace. The HR diagram is synchronized with a plot of the space coordinates of the stars and with imagery of the observed star cluster from the World Wide Telescope.

  4. Flusso regionale studenti di Ateneo

    Website · Github

    An interactive visualization of the inbound, outbound, and stationary flow of Italian students per region, area of study, and sex. Using real data from MIUR, made for the Digital Team of the Italian Government.

  5. Mappa dei quartieri

    Website · Github

    An interactive dashboard to explore and compare different neighborhoods in major Italian cities using data from ISTAT and other public sources. Currently under development.
